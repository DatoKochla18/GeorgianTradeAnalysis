Imports heavily outweigh exports

Total imports are over $720 million more than total exports in 2023 year-to-date
This significant trade deficit puts pressure on currency and foreign exchange reserves

Reliance on few partner countries

Turkey, China and Russia account for over 50% of total imports based on customs data
This signifies Georgia's dependence on just three major trade partners for its key import requirements

Wine as a bright spot for exports

Wine exports have seen growth from countries like Ukraine and China in recent years
As per the data, it contributes over 7.5% to overall exports
Indicates potential to further build the wine industry's export competitiveness

Seasonal patterns in agricultural trade flows

Agricultural imports peak in Spring/Summer months from March through July
Exports are higher later in Fall between September to November
Highlights need for improved storage, processing and logistics infrastructure to smooth flows
